About

Hello, I am a business analytics professional with years of experience in accounting/customer services. In the last three years, working as a tax accountant has given me extensive global experience on data- driven projects in a diverse, cross-functional team, as well as substantial experience working with clients from various backgrounds. My journey in data analytics began during my tenure at Macdonald CPA, a dynamic startup where I worked as an Accounting Analyst. The flexibility and diverse responsibilities in this role allowed me to explore and develop a passion for data analytics. One significant project involved analyzing sales and client data from previous years. I identified that the time and effort spent on individual clients were not yielding proportional returns. Based on my analysis, I recommended a strategic pivot towards engaging more with business owners and small businesses. This led to a 7% increase in sales and a 20% reduction in work hours during the subsequent tax season. This experience was instrumental in shaping my decision to pursue a master's degree in business analytics. Since I was a kid, I have a great passion in Sports and Entertainment and dream about working in the industry. Currently, I am pursuing my master's degree in Business Analytics at UCS Marshall, where I am further refining my expertise in leveraging analytics-driven insights to inform strategic decision- making. In the next career phase, I am eager to work in the entertainment industry, especially in the sports and media areas. I am thrilled to talk to more people from different industries, so please feel free to connect! #Fighton
